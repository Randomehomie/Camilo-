What is Phishing?

Phishing is a type of online scam where deceitful individuals or groups pretend to be trustworthy entities, such as banks or popular websites, to trick people into revealing sensitive information like passwords, credit card numbers, or personal details. They typically do this through deceptive emails, messages, or websites that look legitimate but are actually designed to steal your information. These phishing attempts often contain urgent requests or alarming messages to prompt you to act quickly without thinking carefully, aiming to exploit your trust and catch you off guard. It's important to be cautious and verify the authenticity of any requests for personal information online to protect yourself from falling victim to phishing scams.
